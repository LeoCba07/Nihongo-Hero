## üéÆ About

Nihongo Hero transforms Japanese language study into an engaging RPG adventure. Players progress through story levels, battling enemies by correctly answering Japanese vocabulary and grammar questions. Correct answers deal damage to enemies; mistakes cost you health. It's learning through combat.

**Live Demo:** [nihongohero.quest](https://nihongohero.quest)
